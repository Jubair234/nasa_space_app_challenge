Early Days Of ISS

The ISS was originally intended to be a laboratory, observatory, and factory while providing transportation, maintenance, and a low Earth orbit staging base for possible future missions to the Moon, Mars, and asteroids.

Multinational Oparation

Multinational collaborative project involing five participating space agenices:Nasa(United States), Roscosmos(Russia),JAXA(Japan),ESA (Europe), and CSA(Canada).

Achievements of ISS

Fundamental disease research: Alzheimer's Disease. Parkinson's Disease. Cancer. Asthma. Heart Disease. If any of these conditions has affected your life, so has space station research. .
